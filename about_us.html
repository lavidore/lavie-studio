About Us

Lavie Studio is a gaming platform

Founded in early 2023, Lavie Studio is a Vietnamese company founded by CEO Binh Ca. The company is headquartered in SG, Vietnam and has more than 40 offices worldwide. Today Lavie is a leading interactive entertainment company and provider of 3D engine technology. Lavie operates Fortnite, one of the world’s largest games with over 350 million accounts and 2.5 billion friend connections. Lavie also develops Unreal Engine, which powers the world’s leading games and is also adopted across industries such as film and television, architecture, automotive, manufacturing, and simulation. Through Unreal Engine, Lavie Studio Store, and Lavie Online Services, Lavie provides an end-to-end digital ecosystem for developers and creators to build, distribute, and operate games and other content.
